Prevent Global Warming

  • The biggest way to protect Arctic Wolves is to prevent global warming
  • Global warming creates a chain reaction that leads to the endangerment of the Arctic Wolf

Large Corporations

  • Organizations such as the WWF, work with governments all around the world to stop the destruction of the arctic wolf's habitat
  • If we all work together, we can impact the world in a very big way

Educate

  • Some goals of organizations are to educate people everywhere about wolves, their endangerment, and what can be done to help them
  • Little things such as turning off the lights and conserving energy can be beneficial to stop global warming
